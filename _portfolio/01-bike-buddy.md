Bike Buddy is an affordable and easy-to-install embedded hardware system that enables smart connectivity to conventional bikes. Bike Buddy offers an onboard sensor suite using hall effect sensors for wheel wobble and odometry tracking, and force sensors for brake wear detection, enabling predictive maintenance.
