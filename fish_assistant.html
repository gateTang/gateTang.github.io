As the project manager and the founder of the 'Fish Assistant' Internet of Things project, I worked as the overseer of the project where I took charge of designing electronics, software coding, etc.

As an electronics and software manager, I focused on the IoT aspect of the project where I linked the two concepts together. In total, the product has function such as a temperature check and graph, toggle feed, and toggle change water. The electronics were built around an ESP8266 v2 module connected to components such as the DS18B20 and solenoids. The software was based on a Flask webframe that is connected to a Firebase cloud database and committed to a Heroku server. All of these aspects are linked together using MQTT to communicate within each other.

From this project I learnt a significant amounts of technically skills, especially in the software area and the outcome of the project looks great. I look forward to more similar IoT project to this in the future.
